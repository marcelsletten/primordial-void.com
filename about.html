About

Primordial Void is a label and multidisciplinary collective founded in Lodi, California, in 2018 by Marcel Sletten. Currently based in Athens, Georgia, it is dedicated to publishing digital, physical, and metaphysical editions of music and visual art.

PV has released music by Robert Schneider (the Apples in Stereo, Neutral Milk Hotel), Little Wings, Yellow Swans, Cucina Povera, Twig Harper, Susu Laroche, oxhy, emamouse, whait (More Eaze and Wendy Eisenberg), the Rishis (Elephant 6), Pleasure Systems, April Magazine, Roxane MÃ©tayer, Jeff Witscher, Gobby, Shadows Leap (Surface to Air Missive), and Magic Tuber Stringband, among others.

Active contributors to the label include Sletten, Charles Witherspoon III, Esq., Will Simpson (Crime Friend), Reed Winckler, Chad Beattie (Yes Selma, Orches Limita), Laszlo Horvath, Lawrence Moody (Dividers, Forrest Conifer), Kenny Aguar (Banned 37), aningmal (Fishbowl Prairie), Belgium Limp, Marshall Stacks, Ubaldo, Elazy, Oliver Domingo (Organically Programmed), Ohmu, Harshest Realm, Susu Laroche, and Marc Matchak. Chairs, PV's in-house recording project, lists Sletten, Winckler, Domingo, Nick Bradley, Charlie Enter, and Seth Parks as members.

As a distributor, PV has stocked hard-to-find releases by artists such as Gene Clark, Sun City Girls, Felt, Dean Blunt & Inga Copeland, Basic Channel, and Actress, among others.

As an event series, PV has hosted performers such as Water From Your Eyes, Jeffrey Lewis & the Voltage, Being Dead, DJ Python, More Eaze, MSPAINT, Sword II, Melaina Kol, Chuck Johnson, April Magazine, Godcaster, Gobby, Tredici Bacci, Unrecovery, DJ Lucas, Klark Sound, Sinai Vessel, Kee Avil, Magic Tuber Stringband, Andy Loebs, John Kiran Fernandes & Shane Parish, Immaterial Possession, Bug Bus Piano, Jake Tobin, Tongue Depressor, Theodore Cale Schafer & Sydney Spann, Jack Callahan & Jeff Witscher, and VALISE.
