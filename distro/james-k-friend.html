More enigmatic, autumnal folk brilliance courtesy of our friend and Dean Blunt collaborator Joanne Robertson, who's undoubtedly one of the best in the game right now. The masterful cellist Oliver Coates appears alongside Robertson on three songs, adding a tastefully subdued element to the Glasgow-based artist's lo-fi tracks, which consist of her breathtakingly beautiful vocals and gently fingerpicked guitar. Absolutely top notch, and one of PV's favorite albums of 2025.
